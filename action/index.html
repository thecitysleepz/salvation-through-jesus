Get Involved

You don't have to be perfect to make a difference. All it takes is faith, compassion, and a willingness to act. Join us as we share meals, pray, and help people take the first steps back toward life and family.

Walk With Us

Show up with hope. Walk our city streets, greet people by name, and be a steady presence of love. Email us to learn more about joining our outreach team.

Pray With Us

We gather weekly (times TBD) to intercede for people living on the streets. Commit to praying with us and for us.

Let us know when you can lift up this mission—we'll share prayer prompts.

Share a Meal

Create care packages that deliver both nourishment and dignity:

  • Ready-to-eat meal (shelf stable, easy open)
  • Bottle of water or sports drink
  • Encouraging note with scripture
  • Travel-size hygiene essentials
  • Warm socks or gloves (seasonal)

Reach Out

We'd love to connect, answer questions, and invite you to the streets with us.
