Shop the Mission

Every purchase you make in our store fuels boots-on-the-ground ministry. One hundred percent of the proceeds go directly to meals, care packages, and reunification efforts for people experiencing homelessness.

Where Your Dollars Go

Your support through the store keeps our outreach stocked and ready. When you pick up a hoodie or tee, you are providing tangible care and a reminder of Christ’s love to someone on the streets.

  • Hot meals & meal kits: nourishing food, water, and scripture notes that open the door to prayer.
  • Family reunification: transportation, phone access, and follow-up support to reconnect loved ones.
  • Care on cold nights: emergency blankets, hygiene essentials, and discipleship resources packed with every outing.

We steward every dollar to cover supplies for outreach nights, fuel for our volunteers, and the tools we need to pray, serve, and help people take steps toward home.
