Our Mission

Salvation Through Jesus exists to reach those who are lost and suffering – especially people living on the streets – and meet them with food, prayer, and compassion. One meal can open the door to hope, to family, and to God.

Feed with Love

Every meal is a message: you’re seen, you’re loved, you’re not forgotten.

Restore Connection

A prayer, a phone call, a path home – one step at a time.

Through Jesus

Rescue is more than relief; it’s renewal through Christ.
